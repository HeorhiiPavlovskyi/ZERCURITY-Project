Who is Zercurity?

Zercurity is a hosted service. It provides a secure, managed and centralized platform. It monitors your IT infrastructure from a security and compliance perspective. Zercurity does this in three ways; Using an advanced SQL interface. Allowing you to query your infrastructure just like a database. Application white/blacklisting. Zercurity will automatically block unknown or malicious applications. Compliance checks. Write rules to check that your policies are being adhered too.
